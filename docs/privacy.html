Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ziggy ASL (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Ziggy ASL mobile application.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide: When you set up the app, you may provide onboarding preferences (e.g., learning goals, daily practice targets, motivation). This is stored on your device and used to personalize your experience.

Learning progress: Your lesson progress, streaks, stars, medals, practice history, and related app state are stored on your device. We may sync curriculum content (lessons, signs, and media metadata) from our servers so the app can deliver lessons and practice.

Subscription and payments: If you subscribe to Ziggy ASL Premium, payment and subscription status are handled by Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat). We do not store your payment details; we only receive information necessary to grant access (e.g., that your subscription is active).

Usage and diagnostics: We use Firebase and related infrastructure to deliver app content and may collect anonymous or aggregated data to improve the app (e.g., performance, errors). If we add additional analytics, we will describe that in an update to this policy.

2. How we use your information

We use the information above to provide and improve the app: personalizing your learning path, saving your progress, delivering lessons and practice, tracking streaks and achievements, and managing your subscription access.

3. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed to operate the service (e.g., with Firebase, RevenueCat, Apple, or when required by law) in accordance with their privacy policies.

4. Data storage and security

Your learning progress and preferences are stored on your device. Curriculum and media may be fetched from our cloud services when you use the app. We handle data in line with industry practices and do not retain information longer than necessary to provide the service.

5. Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. To delete data stored on your device, remove the app. For subscription management (cancel, refund), use your Apple ID settings or Apple’s support. If you have questions about your data, contact us (see below).

6. Children

Ziggy ASL is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: maxcascerceri@verizon.net
